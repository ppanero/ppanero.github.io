Who am I?

This guy!

I'm Pablo Panero, a software developer based in Geneva (Switzerland). I'm currently working at CERN, as part of the Digital Repositories team. If you are curious about what we do visit Invenio Software and/or Zenodo.

As for me... I like to build stuff, mostly software, but I am also a huge fan of carpentry. I also like to optimize processes. When you live in a small space, every bit of space counts - the same applies to software -. Yeah... I like my monitoring graphics display green.

Last but not least, I like solving challenges at all scales: From sudokus to complex software architectures.
